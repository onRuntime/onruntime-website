## Introduction

At onRuntime Studio, we place great importance on protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect and use data when you use our services and projects.

## Data collected

### Browsing data

We use two third-party analytics tools to understand the use of our services:

- **Umami Analytics**: A privacy-friendly analytics tool that collects anonymized visit data
- **Google Analytics 4**: For in-depth performance analysis

These services collect anonymized data such as:
- Pages visited
- Visit duration
- Traffic source
- Browser and device information
- Approximate location (country)

These services are subject to their own privacy policies:
- [Google Analytics Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Umami Privacy Policy](https://umami.is/privacy)

### Contact form

If you use our contact forms, we collect:
- Your name
- Your email address
- The content of your message

This information is used to process your request and is kept in our emails to ensure follow-up and quality of our services.

## Use of data

The collected data is used to:
- Improve the user experience of our services
- Analyze the performance of our platforms
- Respond to your contact requests
- Understand how our services are used

## Data retention

### Analytics data
The retention period for analytics data is defined by the policies of Google Analytics and Umami. You can consult their respective policies for more details.

### Contact data
Messages you send us through contact forms are kept in our messaging system to ensure follow-up of our exchanges. You can request the deletion of this data at any time.

## Your rights

In accordance with the GDPR, you have the following rights:
- Right of access to your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to restriction of processing
- Right to object

To exercise these rights or for any questions about the processing of your data, contact us at [admin@onruntime.com](mailto:admin@onruntime.com).

## Cookies and similar technologies

Our services use technical and analytical cookies necessary for their operation and analysis of their use. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

## Data transfer

The analytics services we use may transfer data to servers located outside the EU. These transfers are governed by their respective privacy policies and the safeguards they implement.

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data against any unauthorized access, modification, disclosure, or destruction.

## Modifications

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Modifications take effect upon publication.
